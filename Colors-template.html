Color Loaves

Learn about the different colors of the loaf cats

Red Loaf

The fiery and energetic loaf, often seen zooming around the playroom. Red Loaf is known for its spicy attitude.

Blue Loaf

Calm and chill, Blue Loaf prefers napping in cold corners. Often mistaken for being sad—he’s just vibing.

Pastel Loaf

Soft, dreamy, and adorable. Pastel Loaf is a favorite among players who love cute aesthetics.
